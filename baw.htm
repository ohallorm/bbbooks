Black and White

Paul Volponi (2005)

Synopsis:

This book is about two best friends that do everything together. They are starting varsity basketball players for the local team. One of them is white, Eddie, and the other is black, Marcus. They are heading towards the city championship and the end of their senior year. They get past all of the racial stuff and play well together on the court. They are waiting to see where they will end up in college basketball. Suddenly, their lives change because they need money to buy basketball shoes and go on their senior trip. They decide to steal the money and get into some trouble with the law.

Recommendations:

  • It is a book that will stick in your hands until you are finished reading it. The book is very entertaining and it gives you the perspective of the difference between races. It also shows the troubles of two teenage boys who are living the best part of their lives and they are about ready to make huge strides for their families when bad decisions get in their way. - Eric Wietharn
