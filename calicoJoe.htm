Calico Joe

John Grisham (2012)

Synopsis: It all started with the Chicago Cubs in need of a first basemen. They called up a twenty-one-year-old named Joe Castle.  Joe quickly became the biggest name in baseball and had one of the most remarkable, and memorable, rookie seasons in the history of the game.  He became the idol of the whole country, including a boy named Paul Tracey.  Paul’s dad was a hard-throwing, unforgiving major league pitcher.  When the two ball players met on the baseball diamond, the outcome changed both of their lives forever.

Recommendations:

  • "An amazing blend of baseball, drama and emotion." -Mr. O'Halloran
