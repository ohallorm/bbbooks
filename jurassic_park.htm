Jurassic Park

Author: Michael Crichton

Published: 1991

Genre: Fantasy/Sci-Fi

AR Points: 20

Publisher's Website: Random House

Description: Dinosaurs are cloned for an island resort. Is this safe or just trouble waiting to happen? When experts are called to the island to check it out, they are completely unaware of what they are walking into.

  • The Inside Scoop: "I found this book interesting from the beginning. The action starts off slow but picks up at the end. It brings up front some important issues. Should we reintroduce species that have been extinct? Just because we can do something, should we?"- Mr. Rieschick
  • The Inside Scoop: "A great book. To me, it's as good as the movie. The Lost World book is even better than the movie."- Mr. O'Halloran
