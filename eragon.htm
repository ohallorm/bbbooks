Eragon

Christopher Paolini (2003)

Synopsis:A 15-year-old farm boy is hunting for his family when out of nowhere a mysterious stone appears. The stone soon hatches into a dragon. He finds out about the dragon riders, an extinct race of people and dragons that would enforce the laws of the country. Eragon soon finds out that he is one of them and must fight the empire.
