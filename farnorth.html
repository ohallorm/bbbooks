Far North

Will Hobbs (1996)

Synopsis: Fifteen-year-old Gabe Rogers marvels at the beauty of Canada's Northwest Territories. He hears about the tales of Deadman Valley and a river called the Nahanni. He doesn't know that shortly he will be stranded with his roommate and his roommate's Grandpa in the mysterious valley.
