The Boyfriend League

Rachel Hawthorne(2007)

Synopsis: Baseball was Dani’s life until summer teams brought college baseball players to her small town. Dani and her best friend plot to get the guys. After this summer, baseball and boys are Dani’s life. Some of Dani’s schemes are pretty hair-brained and get her into messes. The question is: Which plot will land her a guy?

Recommendations:

  • This book had me laughing the whole way through it. Anyone who likes a good laugh should read it. It is a book I have seen in the hands of a lot of girls around the school. -Allison Aubert
