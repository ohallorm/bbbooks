Vampire Knight

Matsuri Hino (2006)

Synopsis: Cross Academy is a prestigious boarding school home to two separate classes: the Day Class, and the Night Class. The former is full of normal people, but the latter... is composed entirely of vampires. It's up to the chairman's adopted daughter Yuki and her partner Zero to keep this fact a secret, but Zero's got a few dark secrets of his own...

Recommendations:

  • "Very intense and engaging! The characters and story are wonderfully composed, and the artwork is gorgeous." - Alyssa Johnson
