Wither

Lauren Destenfano(2011)

Synopsis: It’s the 22nd century and they found a cure for cancer.  All of the people who received the antidote live for a very long time, but their kids die at age 20 for girls and 21 for boys.  Rhine is taken from her home and forced to live in a mansion and marry a rich house governor.

Recommendations:"This book was one of my very favorites. It is creepy but thrilling, and I could not put the book down." -Kendra Hutley
