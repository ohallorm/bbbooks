4th Of July

James Patterson and Maxine Paetro (2005)

Synopsis: Police detective Lindsay Boxer made a split-second decision that threatens to tear her life apart. Her self-defense shooting left her on trial and her fate uncertain. To get her mind off of the impending trial, she retreats to a small, quiet town. Unfortunately, the "quiet" part doesn’t last very long, as a rash of murders breaks out. Lindsay must piece together the clues before she becomes yet another victim. This is the fourth book in the best-selling Women's Murder Club series.

Recommendations:

  • Yes, it is called the "Women’s" Murder Club, but that doesn’t mean that one gender will prefer it over the other. This is an exciting thriller in a series of exciting thrillers, and you can jump in at any point and still enjoy it. So, if you are a fan of murder mysteries or suspense, you should try this book. - Mr. O'Halloran
