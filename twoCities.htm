A Tale of Two Cities

Charles Dickens(1859)

This book takes place in London, England and Paris, France during the French Revolution. Two men who look like identical twins but with opposite personalities love the same woman. One of them gets the girl but also must face the guillotine. Will the other man step in to save his rival?

Recommendations:

  • "There are a lot of little coincidences throughout the story that end up becoming connected at the end of the book. There are also a lot of strange minor characters that make the story interesting." - Ms. Birch
