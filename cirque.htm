Cirque Du Freak

Darren Shan (2000)

Synopsis: Cirque Du Freak is the frightening saga of a young boy who visits a freak show with his friend. He discovers that one of the performers is a vampire that has led him to the world of the vampires.

Recommendations:

  • "Cirque Du Freak is a really good book about vampires and supernatural things, so if you like books of vampires, Cirque Du Freak is a good book for you." - Andres Renteria
  • "This book is action packed and sad it has every thing"-Anthony Harpe
