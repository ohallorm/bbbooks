Gregor the Overlander

Author: Suzanne Collins

Published: 2003

Genre: Fantasy/Sci-Fi

AR Points: 8

Publisher's Website: Scholastic

Description: Gregor is a young boy who lives in New York City. One day he stumbles upon a secret world after he falls down into the air duct in the laundry room. He meets a different type of people living down there and gets involved in many different adventures with them. This is the first book in the series and introduces you to so many characters.

  • The Inside Scoop:"Gregor is a funny kid who gets into lots of crazy situations. I liked the book from the first page" - Darrin Flanagan
