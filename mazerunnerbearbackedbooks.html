The Maze Runner

James Dashner (2009)

Synopsis: In a post-apocalyptic world, a young boy named Thomas is sent upward into a maze through a hole in the ground. He has no memory of how or why he is in this situation. When he arrives from the hole he is greeted by fellow "gladers" who have entered this place the same way as he did. He quickly learns that the maze is extremely hazardous beyond the walls, but yearns to find out what lies outside them.

 

AR Points: 15
