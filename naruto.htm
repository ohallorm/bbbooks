Naruto

Author: Masashi Kishimoto

Published: 1999 (in Japan)

Genre: Comedy, Action/Adventure, Comic/Manga, Drama

AR Points: No AR Test

Publisher's Website: Shonen Jump

Description: Naruto is a young ninja boy who is disliked by most everyone in his town. He struggles to succeed in surpassing his aloof rival Sasuke while impressing his crush Sakura. The three are brought together as a team, under the cool-under-pressure Kakashi. How will they fare?

  • The Inside Scoop: "It's very good. Generally, it's mostly humorous, but on the rare dramatic moment, it's equally amazing. It can bring you to tears, laughing or otherwise." - Alyssa Johnson
