A Walk to Remember

Author: Nicholas Sparks, Published: 1999

Synopsis: Landon Carter remembers 1958, his senior year at Beaufort High. Landon's dated a few girls here and there and even claims that he's once been in love, but Jamie Sullivan was the last person he'd ever think of falling in love with. Jamie is a shy, quiet daughter of the town's Baptist minister. She shows Landon the depths of the human heart - and especially the joy and pain of living.

Recommendations:

  • "This book is definitely a tear jerker. It plucks at every single one of your heartstrings." - Brittany Pearl
