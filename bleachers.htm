Bleachers

John Grisham (2011)

Synopsis:

An all-american quarterback, Neely Crenshaw, returns home to Messina, Texas. Like many other former football players, they have come for the passing of their legendary high school coach.  As they sit around the old football field, they relive past games, rehash old relationships and try to decide if they love or hate their old football coach.

Recommendations:

  • "It’s a hard book to put down.  There are plenty of hidden stories. It’s interesting to see a town that lives and dies on high school football, and a coach that pushes his players to their limits, and sometimes even too far." -Mr. O'Halloran
