Missing You

Author: Meg Cabot Published: 2006

Synopsis:Jess gets struck by lightning and gets powers. Her powers allow her to see people who are missing. She finds many kids and soon she is employed by the government. But all along the way, she is in love with a guy named Rob. She gets mentally ill after getting back from overseas and decides not to go back to her home town where Rob is. Instead, she goes to New York. One night she gets an unexpected visitor at her house and then problems begin.

Recommendations:

  • "I would recommend that teenage girls read this book, and the whole series. If you like adventure with a little romance, you'll love this book." - Katie Bertels
