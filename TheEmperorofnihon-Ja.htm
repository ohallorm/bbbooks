The Emperor of Hihon-Ja

John Flanagan(2011)

Horace has traveled to the eastern nation of Nihon-Ja on a vital mission. When his friends recieve no info from him, his friends fear the worst. Finally Alyss, Evanlyn, and Will leave their home land to find him, but when they find him he is in a war with trators of the Emperor along side the Emperor.
